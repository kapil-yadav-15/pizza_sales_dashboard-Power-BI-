# pizza_sales_dashboard-Power-BI-
Designed and developed a two-page interactive Power BI dashboard by extracting and analyzing a structured pizza sales database using SQL aggregation queries.
The project covers complete annual sales data for 2015, focusing on revenue performance, product demand analysis, and sales trend identification to enable data-driven business decision-making.

Page 1 — Sales Overview

Performed daily and monthly trend analysis identifying Friday/Saturday evenings as peak sales periods and July as the highest revenue month
Conducted category-level segmentation revealing Classic pizza contributing maximum sales at 26.91% of total orders
Analyzed size-based distribution showing Large size pizzas dominating at 45.89% of total sales volume

Page 2 — Best & Worst Sellers

Ranked Top 5 and Bottom 5 pizzas across revenue, quantity sold, and total orders using SQL ranking queries
Identified Thai Chicken pizza as highest revenue generator and Classic Deluxe as top seller by quantity
Flagged Brie Carre Pizza as consistent worst performer across all metrics, supporting menu optimization decisions
